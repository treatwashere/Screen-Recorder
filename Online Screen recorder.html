Capture the contents of the entire screen, a window or a selected browser tab and save it as video

Timer


  • Timer delay (seconds)

Instructions


1 Click “Capture” button.
2 Select the window/tab that you want to capture (or entire screen) and grant permission.
3 Go to the area you want to capture and wait until you hear the ding sound.
4 Now the selected area is being recorded.
5 When you finished and want to stop recording, just click the “Stop recording” button.
6 Then, you can preview the video. If you are satisfied with the results, you can download it clicking the “Download video” button. If you are not, you can go back to first step.
